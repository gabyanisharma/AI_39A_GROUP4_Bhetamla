📖 Frequently Asked Questions
How does Bhetamla SOS coordinate my location?
When you trigger the red SOS button, Bhetamla queries your browser GPS coordinates and dynamically formats them into a Google Maps locator link which is emailed to your circle of contacts.
How many contacts can I add?
Bhetamla supports up to 3 safety contacts. This ensures maximum alert efficiency without cluttering emergency responses.
Is my location monitored 24/7?
No, your privacy is fully protected. Location details are only queried dynamically when you manually trigger an SOS alert and are never logged on active servers afterwards.
